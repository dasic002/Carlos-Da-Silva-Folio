Brief

Ergonomic Solutions are a well established manufacturer of hardware to support the Point-Of-Sale, offering mounts, cradles, locks and more to customise the checkout setup. With the market leading towards more intelligent and connected hardware, ES wanted to convert their existing mechanical locks to electronic ones, whilst maintaining the form factor as close as to the original to enable backward compatibility. The locks and framework needed to support an easier “Key” management system, whereby HQ could authorise certain levels of staff to operate assets on the field, as well as logging of lock operations on a cloud system, all the meanwhile making the hardware compact, reliable and easy to operate.
